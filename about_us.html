About Us

History

Founded in 2024, Macsonic Cellphone Repairs began as a specialized repair service for mobile phones and laptops in Kempton Park, Gauteng. The business has expanded from offering repair services to include sales of refurbished laptops and new devices, establishing itself as a comprehensive technology solution provider for individuals and businesses in the local community.

Vision

To become the most trusted mobile phone and laptop repair specialist in the East Rand District, recognised for exceptional technical expertise, quick turnaround times, and comprehensive device solutions.
